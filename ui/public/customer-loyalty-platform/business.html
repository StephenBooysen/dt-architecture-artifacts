Business Perspective

The Customer Loyalty Platform drives customer retention and engagement through personalized rewards, points management, and tier-based benefits programs. It serves as the central hub for loyalty program operations across all customer touchpoints, supporting both transactional and experiential loyalty strategies.

Business Function

Manages comprehensive loyalty programs including points earning, redemption, tier progression, personalized offers, and gamified customer engagement experiences across omnichannel touchpoints.

Business Value

Increases customer lifetime value by 23%, improves retention rates to 87%, and drives 31% higher purchase frequency among active loyalty members versus non-members.

Key Stakeholders

Customer Experience, Marketing, Merchandising, Customer Analytics, Store Operations, Mobile App teams, and 2.8M active loyalty members.

Program Scope

Supports 4 distinct loyalty tiers, 12+ earning categories, 50+ redemption options, and integration with 3rd party coalition partners.
