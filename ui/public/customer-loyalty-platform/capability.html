Capability & Value Stream Perspective

The Customer Loyalty Platform orchestrates enterprise-wide customer engagement capabilities, serving as the master system for loyalty program operations while facilitating cross-functional business capabilities and consuming upstream customer data services. It participates in four critical value streams that drive customer acquisition, retention, revenue growth, and experience optimization.

Core Capabilities (Master)

  • Loyalty Points Management
    Real-time points earning, spending, expiration, and balance tracking across all touchpoints
    Level 5 - Optimized
  • Customer Tier Management
    Automated tier progression (Bronze→Silver→Gold→Platinum) with benefit activation
    Level 4 - Defined
  • Personalized Offers Engine
    AI-driven offer personalization based on behavior, preferences, and tier status
    Level 3 - Managed
  • Gamification & Engagement
    Challenge campaigns, streaks, badges, and social sharing features
    Level 4 - Defined
  • Coalition Partner Management
    Multi-brand point earning/redemption with external partner ecosystem
    Level 3 - Managed

Capabilities Facilitated

  • Customer Analytics
    Provides behavioral data, engagement metrics, and loyalty insights to analytics platforms
    Level 5 - Optimized
  • Marketing Campaign Management
    Enables segmentation by loyalty tier, engagement level, and earning patterns
    Level 4 - Defined
  • Customer Service Integration
    Real-time loyalty status, transaction history, and redemption capabilities in service tools
    Level 4 - Defined
  • Omnichannel Personalization
    Feeds loyalty context to web, mobile, and in-store personalization engines
    Level 3 - Managed

Capabilities Consumed

  • Customer Profile Data
    Consumes unified customer profiles, preferences, and demographic data from CRM
    Level 5 - Optimized
  • Transaction Data Services
    Real-time purchase events from POS, eCommerce, and mobile app platforms
    Level 4 - Defined
  • Product Catalog Services
    Product information for targeted offers, category-based earning rules
    Level 4 - Defined
  • Location Services
    Geolocation data for proximity rewards, store-specific offers
    Level 3 - Managed
  • Payment Processing
    Payment confirmation for points earning validation and redemption processing
    Level 5 - Optimized

Value Stream Participation

Customer Acquisition

Drives new customer enrollment through sign-up bonuses, referral programs, and onboarding campaigns. Contributes 34% of new customer acquisition.

Key Activities: Welcome bonuses, referral tracking, onboarding journeys
Success Metrics: 47% enrollment rate, 72% first-purchase conversion

Customer Retention

Prevents churn through engagement campaigns, win-back offers, and tier benefit utilization. Reduces churn by 23% among active members.

Key Activities: Churn prediction, win-back campaigns, engagement scoring
Success Metrics: 87% retention rate, 31% win-back success rate

Revenue Growth

Drives incremental purchases through targeted offers, tier benefits, and redemption incentives. Generates $47M+ incremental revenue annually.

Key Activities: Offer targeting, tier progression rewards, redemption campaigns
Success Metrics: 31% higher purchase frequency, 18% larger basket size

Customer Experience

Delivers seamless rewards experience across all touchpoints with real-time balance updates and instant redemption capabilities.

Key Activities: Omnichannel integration, instant gratification, personalization
Success Metrics: 4.7/5 program satisfaction, 92% digital engagement
